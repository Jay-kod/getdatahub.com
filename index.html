Frequently Asked Questions

WHAT IS GETDATA HUB?

GETDATA HUB is a convenient platform for purchasing data, airtime, and cable subscriptions across Nigeria. Available on both our website and app, we provide seamless access on a variety of devices, ensuring you can easily manage your services anytime, anywhere.

WHAT ARE YOUR HOURS OF OPERATION?

We are available 24/7 to serve you better, ensuring seamless service at all times. However, during scheduled server maintenance or unforeseen technical issues, you may experience temporary difficulty or limited access to our website or app. We appreciate your understanding and patience.

IS THIS PLATFORM LEGITIMATE?

GETDATA HUB is a fully registered Nigerian company, operating with official licensing. We are committed to providing 100% legitimate services to all our customers, ensuring your trust and satisfaction.

DO YOU HAVE AN APP, AND WHERE CAN I FIND IT?

Our mobile app is available for direct download in APK format. Simply visit our website at www.getdata.com.ng to download and install the app on your device. We also serve IOS users through the website and web app. We're here to provide you with seamless access to our services.

HOW DO I FUND MY WALLET?

To fund your account, simply log in to your account, then click on "Fund My Account." From there, choose from our various funding options and follow the prompts to complete the process. Alternatively, you can purchase a coupon code directly from one of our agents.

HOW DO I BUY DATA OR AIRTIME

To buy airtime or data on a data site, log in to your account and go to the airtime or data section. Select your mobile network provider, enter the phone number, and choose the amount or data plan you want. Complete the payment, and the airtime or data will be instantly credited to your number.
